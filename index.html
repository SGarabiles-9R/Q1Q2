Up

Pixar Studios [2009]

In the 2000 movie "Up", a grieving widower named Carl Fredricksen fulfills a lifelong dream of traveling to South America by tying thousands of balloons to his house and flying there. Along the way, he inadvertently takes a young boy named Russell, a golden retriever named Dug, and a rare bird named Kevin on an adventure that challenges his views on life and friendship. As Carl learns to let go of his past and embrace new relationships, he discovers that the real adventure is in the bonds he forms and the people he touches.
